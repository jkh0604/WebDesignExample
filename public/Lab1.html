Lab 1

This is a simple lab to setup the directory structure for the later labs.

The server is a copy of the Labs from the previous weeks. In this lab you will create a copy of the Book folder and rename it to with with your Assignment 1 and Assignment 3 content.

Copy the book application now and rename it to match your Assignment 1 and Assignment 3 content

Rename each of the files - bookController.js, bookData.json, and bookModel.json to match your content

Update the bookController.js and bookModel.js to match your content. In both, you only need methods to return all content. (ie. you dont need a method to delete content or get content by id or category)

Copy your appData.json from Assignment 3 into the appData.json

In the /routes directory, copy Book.js and rename it appropriately. Make note of the commented line in the router. Since you only have a method to get all content, this commented line is all you need.

Finally, update app.js - create a app.use for your own content. Be sure to include the appropriate router.

To test, open the path you created and verify your json data is returned. If it did not, then you need to debug!
